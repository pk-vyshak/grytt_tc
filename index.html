Privacy Policy for Grytt

Effective date: October 19, 2025

Grytt is a personalised workout logging app where users can log workouts and, if they choose to connect as friends, view each other’s workout logs. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for login and communication)
  • Date of birth (to personalise experience and verify eligibility)
  • Training mode (strength, endurance, etc.)
  • Workout frequency (days per week)
  • Gender (to personalise insights)
  • Consent for notifications (opt-in/out)
  • Social data (friends list and workout visibility you choose to share)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve app features
  • Personalise training insights
  • Enable social features with friends
  • Send notifications if you opted in
  • Maintain account security and comply with laws

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared with:

  • Your friends (if you enable sharing of workout logs)
  • Service providers (hosting, support) under confidentiality
  • Authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We keep your information while your account is active. If you stop using Grytt and want your information handled differently, you can contact us at the email below.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your details in the app
  • Control workout log visibility
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Contact support if you have privacy-related requests

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Grytt is not for children under 13 (or the legal minimum age in your country).

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the “Effective date” when changes are made.

9. Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact us at: gryttfitnes@gmail.com
